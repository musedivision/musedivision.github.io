Elevationclose

Vary the height of your camera position for each room. Subtle height changes can make a room appear larger. Using ladders, especially for outdoor photography can make vast improvements.

Tip 2: Flowmore_vert

click to learn

Flowclose

Walk your buyers through your home with photography. Create this flow by incorporating points of interest in multiple shots i.e. include part of the dining table when shooting the kitchen, then again when shooting a living area. This allows buyers to visualise the layout and how living areas relate.

Tip 3: Herosmore_vert

click to learn

Herosclose

Identify your 'Hero Pics' that will be used on your Street Front Sign. Online Listing and Brochures (often is shot of the living area and front of house). Dont be afraid to push furniture around, arrange accessories and tweak your camera position often in order to make the most of your room.
