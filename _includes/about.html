The White Rabbit


Easily found on the fringes of Auckland’s bustling city life, The White Rabbit on the Park is quickly becoming a local favourite.

Nestled in the iconic Victoria Park Market, The White Rabbit on the Park prides itself on serving quality meals and drinks coupled with exceptional service. The restaurant serves up a bistro-styled seasonal menu infusing modern techniques into classic dishes. Be it a family get-together or after-work drinks, there’s something for everyone at The White Rabbit on the Park.

Coined ‘The Hutch’ by its friendly team, the bar is fully stocked with a wide variety of local and international beer and wine. Or, for something a little stronger, the bar staff are happy to serve beautiful cocktails and quality spirits. These drinks can be enjoyed from the leisure of the heated Beer Garden, a perfect way to relax and unwind with friends.

For those seeking a more private experience, The White Rabbit on the Park offers one of Auckland’s premier private dining rooms. The multifaceted nature of the space allows for exclusive use for just about any occasion, from a small private dinner to a large party.

So, what are you waiting for? Come on down to The White Rabbit on the Park for good music, and great food and drinks from our friendly staff. It may just be your new local.
