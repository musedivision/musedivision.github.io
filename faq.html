FAQ

  • Who is Privateer

    Privateer is a modern and transparent approach to selling real estate, saving its clients over $22,000 on average in commission fees. Privateer gives you the confidence, products and services to execute a successful sale campaign yourself.

  • Why choose Privateer over an agent?

    Trade Me has revolutionised the way we find property. It is a database of all properties on the market irrespective of which company is selling it. Whilst you remain in control of the total process Privateer will walk you through from photography to a signed sale and purchase agreement at a fraction of the cost.

  • How does Privateer work?

    We work with you to sell your home. Weather you engage us to host a bunch of open homes, put up a street front sign or discuss a recent offer, we are on your side.

  • Some agents claim to have a buyer in hand?

    Heard it before all before! If their buyer is interested in buying property they will be looking on Trade Me. Most motivated buyers will have registered alert systems set to identify new properties on the market, in any given area and price range.

  • Can I use an agent and Privateer?

    Yes you can. It is common to see a property listed with multiple agents. We suggest you list your property using our services and engage an agent once your campaign has started to ensure ownership amongst buyers.

  • What do I say when a buyer asks “how much?”

    It depends. Your sales strategy (Auction, Asking Price, Enquiries over etc) will determine the right answer. We can prepare you in how to deal with most circumstances in a controlled and effective manner.

  • Am I cut-out for selling my property myself?

    Selling property independent of a real estate agent is easy. We can help you put in place a strategy which is most suited to you and guide you through the process from start to finish.

  • How long does it take to list our property on Trade Me?

    As soon as photography is in hand, we can have you listed and ready for sale in a day or two.

  • How are your fees so low?

    Fewer overheads. Large High St offices are not required to sell your home. We simple provide you what you need to sell your home, nothing more.

  • Will you do the negotiating for me?

    Short answer is no. We can prepare and direct you in your negotiation process as well as suggest strategies and techniques to ensure the best outcome.

  • Are floorplans important?

    It is proved, people spend 35% more time on listings with a floorplan than those that don’t.

  • Any hidden charges?

    No. All of Privateers products and services have a set price.

  • I’m worried about showing my home

    Let us show it for you. Our professional service will welcome buyers through your home and will provide you a detailed registration of attendees after the open home.

  • Is the legal stuff to hard?

    Not at all! The legal element is only a small part of selling your home and in the vast majority of cases a very straight forward process. We can provide recommended conveyancers to service your legal requirements.

  • How much is my house worth?

    We will provide you all the sales data in your area so you can compare your house to others recently sold. If you’re thinking about putting your house on the market, we suggest you take an interest in any current local sales and note how they are being sold and for how much. This will give you a better understanding and realistic approach to setting your price.

  • How do buyers respond to properties sold by owners?

    Refreshingly surprised. Most buyers prefer to deal directly with the owner of a property.
